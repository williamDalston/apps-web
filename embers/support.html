Embers Support

Embers keeps your closest friendships from fading. Every friend is an ember — contact keeps it glowing, silence lets it dim.

How warmth works

Each friend has a cadence (weekly for your inner circle, monthly for close friends, quarterly for keep-in-touch — all adjustable). The ember fades as the cadence window passes and reglows when you log contact.

Not getting reminders?

Embers sends at most one notification a day. Check Settings → Notifications → Embers on your iPhone, and the daily check-in time inside the app's settings.

Restore purchases

Settings → Unlock Embers Plus → Restore purchases.

Export your data

Settings → Export everything (JSON). Your data lives only on your device.

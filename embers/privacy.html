Embers Privacy

Your friendships are none of our business.

Embers collects no data. There is no account, no server, no analytics, and no third-party code. Friends you add — including any names, photos, and birthdays pulled from Contacts — are stored only on your device. Nothing is ever uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes everything.

Embers uses the system contact picker, which means the app can only see the specific people you choose — never your full address book.

Purchases are processed by Apple; we never see payment details.
